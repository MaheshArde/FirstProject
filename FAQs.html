General Travel Information

What is the best time to visit India?

The best time to visit India depends on the region you plan to visit. Generally, October to March offers pleasant weather across most parts of the country. The winter months (December-February) are ideal for visiting southern India, while the Himalayan regions are best visited from April to June and September to November.

Do I need a visa to visit India?

Yes, most foreign nationals require a visa to enter India. You can apply for an e-Visa online through the Indian government's official website. The process typically takes 3-5 business days. Some nationalities are eligible for visa on arrival, while others must apply in advance at an Indian embassy or consulate.

What vaccinations are required for travel to India?

While no vaccinations are officially required for entry (unless coming from a yellow fever endemic country), we recommend being up-to-date on routine vaccines and considering vaccinations for Hepatitis A, Typhoid, and Tetanus. Consult with your doctor or a travel medicine specialist at least 4-6 weeks before your trip.

Booking & Payments

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit and debit cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express), PayPal, and bank transfers. For bookings within India, we also accept UPI payments and select digital wallets. All transactions are secured with SSL encryption for your safety.

What is your cancellation policy?

Our cancellation policy varies by service provider. Generally, cancellations made more than 30 days before arrival receive a full refund, 15-30 days receive a 50% refund, and less than 15 days are non-refundable. Some special offers or peak season bookings may have different policies, which will be clearly stated during the booking process.

Can I modify my booking after confirmation?

Yes, most bookings can be modified, subject to availability and any rate differences. Changes to dates or services may incur modification fees depending on the provider's policy. Please contact our customer support team at least 72 hours before your scheduled service to request changes.

Travel Services

Do you provide airport transfers?

Yes, we can arrange airport transfers at most major airports in India. Transfers can be booked as part of your package or separately. Our representatives will meet you at arrivals with a name sign and assist with your luggage. Please provide your flight details at least 48 hours before arrival.

Are your tour guides licensed?

All our tour guides are officially licensed by the Indian Ministry of Tourism and have extensive knowledge of their regions. They speak fluent English and many other languages. Specialized guides (for wildlife, heritage sites, etc.) have additional certifications in their respective fields.

What safety measures are in place for travelers?

Your safety is our priority. All our partners adhere to strict safety standards. Vehicles are regularly maintained and insured, hotels are vetted for safety features, and guides are trained in first aid. We provide 24/7 emergency support and recommend all travelers purchase comprehensive travel insurance.
